# 🎯 PML Career Assistance — Learner Journey Guide

> **Audience:** Learners enrolled in Airtribe's AI-First Product Management (PML) Program  
> **Maintained by:** Program Manager, Airtribe  
> **Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## 📌 Overview

Career Assistance at Airtribe is a structured, milestone-gated process designed to ensure every learner who enters it is truly job-ready. It is not automatic — you need to **earn your way in** by qualifying your Capstone Project.

Here's the high-level journey:

```
Capstone Evaluation → Profile Optimization → Profile Approval
       → Mock Interviews → Priority Hiring Pool
```

---

## 🗺️ The Complete Career Assistance Journey

---

### ✅ STEP 1 — Capstone Evaluation & Qualification

**What happens:**
Your Capstone Project is evaluated 1:1 by a mentor and graded out of **10**.

**Qualification Criteria:**
- You need a minimum score of **7/10 (70%)** to qualify for Career Assistance.

**What happens after you qualify:**
- You are added to a **private Slack channel** dedicated to your cohort's career services.
  - Example channel name: `pro-career-services-pmlc23`
- This is where all career assistance communication, updates, and announcements will happen.

> ⚠️ **Did not qualify?** You will need to revisit and resubmit your Capstone. Reach out to your Program Manager for guidance.

---

### ✅ STEP 2 — Profile Optimization

Profile Optimization consists of **5 dedicated group sessions** covering three areas: Resume, Portfolio, and LinkedIn. All sessions are live and mentor-led.

---

#### 📄 Resume Optimization (3 Sessions)

| Session | What Happens |
|--------|--------------|
| **Session 1** | Mentor covers the **Dos & Don'ts** of a PM resume — ideal structure, must-have sections, ideal resume length based on years of experience, and what recruiters look for. |
| **Session 2** | Live Zoom session where the mentor goes through **each learner's resume one by one** and gives personalised feedback on what changes need to be made. |
| **Session 3** | Mentor **reviews each resume again** to verify whether the suggested changes have been implemented correctly. |

---

#### 💼 Portfolio Optimization (1 Session)

- Mentor walks through a **Notion-based Portfolio Optimization Doc**
- Explains what to include in your PM portfolio
- Provides **templates** that can be duplicated and filled
- Covers Dos & Don'ts
- Reviews a few learner portfolios live and gives feedback
- Shares examples of **ideal PM portfolios** for reference

---

#### 🔗 LinkedIn Optimization (1 Session)

- Follows a similar format to the Portfolio session
- Mentor covers what a recruiter-friendly LinkedIn profile looks like
- Reviews and gives feedback on learner profiles live

---

### ✅ STEP 3 — Profile Submission & Approval

Once all **5 sessions are complete**, here's what happens next:

1. **Submit your profiles** (Resume + Portfolio + LinkedIn) through your **Airtribe Dashboard**
2. Profiles are **reviewed and verified** by internal mentors
3. Once **all three profiles are approved**, you are added to the **`job-opportunities-product`** Slack channel
   - This is where the Career Assistance team regularly posts **job opportunities** across companies and roles

> ⚠️ **Important:** Mock Interviews begin **only after all three profiles are approved**. Do not request mocks before this step is complete.

---

### ✅ STEP 4 — Mock Interviews

Airtribe has **3 curated mock interviews**, each designed to simulate real PM interview rounds:

| # | Mock Interview Type |
|---|-------------------|
| 1 | 🎨 Product Design |
| 2 | 📊 Tech, Analytics & AI |
| 3 | 🗣️ Behavioural Questions |

---

#### How the Mock Process Works

- Mocks are **sequential** — you can only move to the next mock after qualifying the current one
- Each mock is evaluated by a mentor across specific criteria — you are marked **Qualified** or **Not Qualified** on each criterion
- You qualify a mock only when you clear **all criteria** for that mock
- You have **unlimited attempts** for each mock

---

#### ⏱️ Turnaround Time (TAT) & Guidelines

| Scenario | What To Do |
|----------|-----------|
| ✅ **You qualify a mock** | Wait **3 days**, then request the next mock via the Dashboard |
| ❌ **You disqualify a mock** | Wait **7 days**, then request the same mock again via the Dashboard |
| ❌❌ **You disqualify twice consecutively** | You are eligible for **1:1 Mentorship Support** — reach out to your Program Manager directly |

---

#### 📲 How to Request a Mock Interview

```
Dashboard → Career Section → Mock Interviews → Request a Mock
→ Team will reach out to you with available slots
```

> ⚠️ **Please Note:** Calling or messaging multiple times to check mock availability **does not speed up scheduling** and disrupts the process. All requests must be raised **only through the Dashboard** following the defined TAT.

---

### ✅ STEP 5 — Priority Hiring Pool 🏆

Once you have **qualified all three mock interviews**, you enter the **Priority Hiring Pool**.

**What this means for you:**
- You receive **regular, timely updates** on job applications and opportunities
- The Career Assistance team **actively pushes your profile** to hiring partners
- You get **priority visibility** over non-qualified learners in referral and placement conversations

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

---

**Q1. When does Career Assistance start?**
> It starts after your Capstone Project is evaluated and you score 7/10 or above.

---

**Q2. What if I score below 7 in my Capstone?**
> You will not be eligible for Career Assistance until you resubmit and qualify. Reach out to your Program Manager for next steps.

---

**Q3. Can I skip a profile optimization session?**
> The sessions are structured and sequential. Skipping sessions may delay your profile approval. It is strongly recommended to attend all 5 sessions.

---

**Q4. Can I start mock interviews before my profiles are approved?**
> No. Mocks begin **only after all three profiles (Resume, Portfolio, LinkedIn) are approved** by the internal mentors.

---

**Q5. How many attempts do I get for each mock?**
> You have **unlimited attempts** for each mock. However, there are mandatory waiting periods between attempts (3 days after qualifying, 7 days after disqualifying).

---

**Q6. Can I appear for Mock 2 before qualifying Mock 1?**
> No. Mocks are **sequential**. You must qualify Mock 1 before being eligible for Mock 2, and so on.

---

**Q7. What happens if I fail the same mock twice in a row?**
> You become eligible for **1:1 mentorship support**. Reach out to your Program Manager directly to get guidance and prepare better.

---

**Q8. How do I request a mock interview?**
> Go to your **Airtribe Dashboard → Career Section → Mock Interviews → Request a Mock**. The team will get back to you with slot options.

---

**Q9. What is the Priority Hiring Pool?**
> It's a special group of learners who have qualified all 3 mocks. The Career Assistance team actively advocates for these learners with hiring partners and gives them first access to opportunities.

---

**Q10. Who do I reach out to if I'm stuck or have questions?**
> Reach out to your **Program Manager, Smaranika** for any guidance related to the career assistance process.

## ✅ Dos and Don'ts

### ✅ Dos
- Attend **all 5 profile optimization sessions** actively
- Implement resume feedback **before Session 3**
- Submit profiles through the **Dashboard only**
- Follow the **TAT strictly** before raising mock requests
- Use the **Dashboard to raise all mock interview requests**
- Reach out to your PM if you disqualify a mock **twice in a row**

### ❌ Don'ts
- Don't skip profile sessions or submit unrevised profiles
- Don't request mocks before all profiles are approved
- Don't call or message repeatedly to check mock availability — it doesn't help and disrupts the process
- Don't try to jump mocks — the sequence is fixed
- Don't wait until the last minute to start your Capstone seriously — your score determines your entry
