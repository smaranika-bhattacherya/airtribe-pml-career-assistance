# PML Career Assistance Program
### Airtribe | AI-First Product Management

---

## Overview

Career Assistance at Airtribe is a structured, milestone-gated process. Eligibility is earned through academic performance, not granted automatically. Learners must progress through each stage sequentially — no stage may be skipped.

The program consists of five stages: Capstone Evaluation, Profile Optimization, Profile Approval, Mock Interviews, and Priority Hiring Pool.

---

## Stage 1 — Capstone Evaluation

The Capstone Project is evaluated in a 1:1 session with a mentor and graded on a scale of 10.

**Minimum qualifying score: 7/10 (70%)**

- Learners who meet or exceed this threshold are added to a private cohort Slack channel (e.g. `#pro-career-services-pmlc23`) and proceed to Stage 2.
- Learners who do not qualify must revise and resubmit their Capstone. Contact your Program Manager for resubmission guidance.

---

## Stage 2 — Profile Optimization

Learners attend five structured, mentor-led group sessions covering Resume, Portfolio, and LinkedIn.

### Resume (3 Sessions)

| Session | Title | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Session 1 | Dos and Don'ts Workshop | Covers ideal PM resume structure, required sections, length guidelines based on experience level, and recruiter priorities. |
| Session 2 | Live Review Round | Each learner's resume is reviewed individually on a live Zoom call. The mentor provides specific, personalised feedback on required changes. |
| Session 3 | Changes Verification | The mentor re-reviews each resume to confirm that all feedback from Session 2 has been correctly implemented. |

### Portfolio (1 Session)

| Session | Title | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Session 4 | Portfolio Optimization Session | Mentor walks through a Notion-based Portfolio Optimization document. Covers what to include, provides templates, shares Dos and Don'ts, reviews learner portfolios live, and presents examples of strong PM portfolios. |

### LinkedIn (1 Session)

| Session | Title | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Session 5 | LinkedIn Optimization Session | Mentor reviews what a recruiter-friendly LinkedIn profile entails and reviews learner profiles live, with direct feedback. |

---

## Stage 3 — Profile Submission and Approval

After completing all five sessions, learners submit their profiles for internal review.

**Submission path:** Airtribe Dashboard > Career Section > Submit Profiles

All three profiles — Resume, Portfolio, and LinkedIn — must be submitted and independently approved by internal mentors.

Upon approval of all three profiles:
- The learner is added to `#job-opportunities-product`, the channel where the Career Assistance team posts curated job opportunities.
- The learner becomes eligible to request Mock Interviews.

> **Note:** Mock Interviews will not be scheduled until all three profiles have received explicit approval. Requests made prior to full approval will not be processed.

---

## Stage 4 — Mock Interviews

Airtribe conducts three sequential mock interviews, each simulating a distinct PM interview format. Learners must qualify each mock before advancing to the next.

### Mock Interview Format

| Mock | Type |
|------|------|
| Mock 1 | Product Design |
| Mock 2 | Technology, Analytics and AI |
| Mock 3 | Behavioural Questions |

### Turnaround Time (TAT)

| Outcome | Waiting Period | Next Action |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Qualified | 3 days | Request the next mock via the Dashboard |
| Disqualified (first attempt) | 7 days | Request the same mock again via the Dashboard |
| Disqualified (two consecutive attempts) | — | Eligible for 1:1 mentorship support. Contact your Program Manager directly. |
| No-show on 2 consecutive scheduled mocks | 15 days (access paused) | Mock Interview section on the Dashboard will be paused. Access is restored after 15 days. |

### How to Request a Mock Interview

1. Navigate to the Airtribe Dashboard
2. Go to Career Section > Mock Interviews
3. Select "Request a Mock"
4. The team will respond with available slot options

> **Important:** All mock interview requests must be submitted exclusively through the Dashboard. Contacting the team via call or direct message to check availability does not expedite the process and is discouraged.

---

## Stage 5 — Priority Hiring Pool

Learners who qualify all three mock interviews are inducted into the Priority Hiring Pool — the highest tier of Airtribe's career support infrastructure.

**Benefits include:**

- Active advocacy by the Career Assistance team with hiring partners
- Priority visibility in referral and placement conversations
- Timely and regular updates on the status of job applications
- First access to curated role postings in `#job-opportunities-product`

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. When does the Career Assistance process begin?**
Career Assistance is triggered by Capstone qualification, not by a fixed calendar date. Once a learner scores 7/10 or above on their Capstone evaluation, the process initiates automatically.

---

**Q2. What happens if I score below 7 on my Capstone?**
Learners who do not meet the qualifying threshold are ineligible to proceed until they resubmit and qualify. Reach out to your Program Manager for resubmission instructions and timelines.

---

**Q3. Are the profile optimization sessions mandatory?**
Attendance at all five sessions is strongly recommended. The sessions are structured sequentially, and missing sessions may result in delayed profile approval. Incomplete or unrevised profiles will not be approved.

---

**Q4. Can I request a Mock Interview before my profiles are approved?**
No. Mock Interviews are accessible only after all three profiles — Resume, Portfolio, and LinkedIn — have been reviewed and formally approved by internal mentors. Requests made before this point will not be entertained.

---

**Q5. Is there a limit on the number of mock interview attempts?**
There is no cap on the total number of attempts. However, mandatory waiting periods apply: 3 days after qualifying a mock, and 7 days after a disqualification, before a new request can be submitted.

---

**Q6. Can I appear for Mock 2 before qualifying Mock 1?**
No. The mock interview sequence is fixed. Learners must qualify Mock 1 (Product Design) before becoming eligible for Mock 2 (Technology, Analytics and AI), and must qualify Mock 2 before proceeding to Mock 3 (Behavioural Questions).

---

**Q7. What support is available if I fail the same mock twice in a row?**
Learners who are disqualified in two consecutive attempts for the same mock become eligible for dedicated 1:1 mentorship. Contact your Program Manager directly to schedule this support before your next attempt.

---

**Q8. What is the correct process for requesting a mock interview?**
All requests must be submitted via the Airtribe Dashboard under Career Section > Mock Interviews. The Career Assistance team will share available slots in response. Requests made outside the Dashboard — including via phone or direct message — are not valid and will not be processed.

---

**Q9. What is the Priority Hiring Pool and what does it offer?**
The Priority Hiring Pool is a select group of learners who have successfully qualified all three mock interviews. Learners in this pool receive active support from the Career Assistance team, including direct advocacy with hiring partners, referral priority, and first access to curated job postings.

---

**Q10. How does the Career Assistance team share job opportunities?**
Approved job opportunities are shared in the `#job-opportunities-product` Slack channel. Learners gain access to this channel only after all three profiles have been approved.

---

**Q11. What if I am unable to attend a profile optimization session?**
If you are unable to attend a session, inform your Program Manager in advance. However, since sessions include live reviews and real-time feedback, the impact of absence is significant and may delay your profile approval.

---

**Q16. What happens if I schedule a mock interview but do not attend?**
If a learner fails to attend a scheduled mock interview on two consecutive occasions without prior notice, their Mock Interview section on the Airtribe Dashboard will be paused for 15 days. During this period, no new mock requests can be submitted. Access is automatically restored once the 15-day pause has elapsed.

---

**Q17. Is there a way to avoid the 15-day pause for non-attendance?**
If you are unable to attend a scheduled mock, notify your Program Manager in advance. Proactive communication may be taken into consideration. Repeated no-shows without prior notice will result in the 15-day access pause as a standard consequence.

---

**Q12. How long does the profile approval process take after submission?**
Turnaround time for profile approval is not fixed and depends on the review queue. Learners will be notified through the cohort Slack channel once their profiles are approved. Do not submit mock requests while approval is pending.

---

**Q13. Can I update my profiles after they have been approved?**
Minor updates are generally permissible. However, significant changes to an approved profile should be discussed with your mentor or Program Manager to ensure the profile remains at the required standard.

---

**Q14. Who is the right point of contact for Career Assistance queries?**
All Career Assistance queries should be directed to your Program Manager via the cohort Slack channel. For matters related to 1:1 mentorship after two consecutive mock disqualifications, contact your Program Manager directly.

---

**Q15. Is the Career Assistance program available to all PML learners?**
Career Assistance is available to all learners enrolled in the AI-First Product Management program who meet the Capstone qualification threshold. Learners who do not qualify their Capstone are not eligible until resubmission and qualification.

## Key Guidelines

**Learners are expected to:**
- Attend all profile optimization sessions and implement feedback before verification sessions
- Submit profiles only through the Airtribe Dashboard
- Adhere strictly to the TAT before raising mock interview requests
- Use the Dashboard for all mock interview requests without exception

**Learners must not:**
- Request mock interviews before all three profiles are approved
- Attempt to bypass the sequential mock interview order
- Contact the team via phone or direct message to check mock availability
- Submit profiles that have not incorporated session feedback
